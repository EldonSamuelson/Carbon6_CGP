About

from this, your city could more easily access grants for nature building, and become a major contributor to the efforts against climate change.

Ubiquitous and OpenSource Production

We use software and data products to enhance our own data collection and ensure output. With QGIS, Python, Oracle and other flexible applications, we are able to collate and analyse data to create meaningful indices relating to carbon sequestration and environmental health. You can use this indices to triage your city for improvement, and create personalised action plans that our mapping and database capabilities can facilitate.

Database Querying

Our Database Informatics use an SQL and Python machine to query the data we collect from our surveys and translate it into meaningful insight via our various environmental indicators. The inforamtion can be viewed in a marketable output and table, and can be intelligently queried for location or attribute specific data to suit your needs and efficiently capture statistics on areas of environmental deprivation. Tools for comparing multiple locations and exploring the expansion into new greenspaces are also in development.

Location

Our intelligent mapping systems use layer selection and lightbox facilities to bring together composites and comparisons of the location data we collect. The intuitive design allows for marketable data visualisations of all of our spatial data which we use to create simple and scientifically recognised indicators. Our high resolution data imagery products are visualised so you don't get lost in the maps, and we provide profiles of each of the greenspaces selected for a closer look at their variation and significance in their geographical context.
