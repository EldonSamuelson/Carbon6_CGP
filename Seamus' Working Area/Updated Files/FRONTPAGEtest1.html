WHAT WE PROVIDE

Using spatial tools and web programming, we are able to collate and analyse data to develop a carbon based environmental deprivation index. This index can be used to create zone wise action plans that our mapping and database capabilities can facilitate.

Database Querying

Our database model provides meaningful insight of various carbon sequestration based environmental indicators. This information is beneficial to compare different areas within the city of Edinburgh and identifying factors responsible for environmental deprivation.

Location

Our spatial data products provide visualisation of a variety of factors contributing to Edinburgh's potential to sequester carbon. Greenspace case studies contain high resolution imageries to aid in better understanding of Edinburgh's greenspaces.
